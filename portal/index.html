Welcome !!

TestYourSkills.com is a online programming portal which collaborates with the academic syllabus of colleges in India, thereby connecting the much-needed efficitient programmers with the new Startups & MNC's.

Awesome Features

Let's have a look at the some of the amazing features of our portal !

Programming Portal

This would be a programming portal where the students can improve their skills & compette with students across the country.

Integration With Colleges

We would tie-up with colleges, so that we would be able to provide programming challenges according to their syllabus as needed.

Easy Monitoring

The staffs of respective colleges & department can add their own problems & test-cases to their student's assignment.

Performace Reports

The head of the departments would be able to monitor the performance of the students.

Open Challenges

AApart from challenges posted by their staffs, the students would be able to participate in open-programming contests and learn new technologies.

Tie Up With Startups & MNC

We would be able to tie up with the emerging startups & MNC's who are in need of efficient programmers around the globe.
